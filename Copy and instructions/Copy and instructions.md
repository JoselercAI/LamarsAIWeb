SECCIÓN: FAQ
TITULAR DE SECCIÓN
Questions
Is it really free?
Yes. LAMARS is free during our beta period. No credit card, no trial expiration. We’ll introduce
paid plans later with advanced features — but the core daily briefing will always have a free tier.
Will LAMARS send messages to my clients?
Never. LAMARS only reads your conversations to understand what’s happening with your
leads. It never sends anything on your behalf. You stay 100% in control of every message.
Do I need to replace my CRM?
No. LAMARS works alongside whatever you already use — even if that’s just WhatsApp and
your memory. No migration, no new system to learn. Just connect and start seeing your daily
briefing.
Does my brokerage need to approve this?
No. Agents can start individually. You connect your own WhatsApp and get your own daily
briefing. If your brokerage wants team features later, they can connect separately.
How long does setup take?
15 minutes. Download the app, scan a QR code to connect WhatsApp, and mark any personal
contacts that aren’t leads. By the next morning, your first briefing is ready.
Is this only for Dubai?
We’re launching in Dubai first, designed for the WhatsApp-first real estate market here. If you’re
an agent working in Dubai or the UAE, this is built for you.
